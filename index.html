Why Amanda is different

A serious musician who teaches the person, not just the piece.

Lots of local music lessons promise convenience. Amanda offers something more personal: strong musicianship, patient teaching, classroom-trained care and a first conversation with the actual teacher before you commit.

01Musical standards without pressure

Grade 8 ABRSM Piano Performance, Grade 8 ABRSM Violin Performance and graded theory give Amanda the depth to teach secure technique, reading, listening and expression from the start.

02Confidence comes first

Children and adult beginners often arrive worried about mistakes. Amanda keeps the pace clear and encouraging, so learners can feel safe enough to try, practise and improve.

03More than playing notes

As a former Uppingham music scholar with ensemble, performance and Pro Corda experience, Amanda understands how music builds listening, discipline, patience, confidence and self-belief.

04Teacher-level care

QTS and PGCE training mean lessons are planned around the learner in front of her: age, confidence, concentration, home practice and what will make progress feel possible.
