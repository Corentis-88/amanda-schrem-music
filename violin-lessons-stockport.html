A careful start

The early weeks matter on violin.

Violin can be exciting, but it can also feel unforgiving at the beginning. Amanda keeps the basics manageable, building posture, bow hold, sound and listening skills before rushing into harder pieces.

  • Posture, bow hold and sound production taught patiently.
  • Encouraging lessons for children who need time to settle.
  • Exam routes available when useful, never treated as the only goal.
  • Lessons planned around confidence, consistency and home practice.
  • Ensemble experience helps Amanda teach listening, timing and musical awareness from the beginning.
